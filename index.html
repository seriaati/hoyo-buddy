Commands

What can Hoyo Buddy do?

Take a peak at some of the most popular commands in Hoyo Buddy!

Centralize all your accounts effortlessly in one location. Enjoy three intuitive methods for adding accounts quickly and seamlessly.

/accounts

Explore anything related to Genshin Impact and Honkai: Star Rail with ease. Perfect for introducing game concepts to newcomers.

/search

Access your real-time notes with added reminder features, facilitating tracking of resin, daily commissions, and other tasks.

/notes

Condense all your owned characters into one comprehensive card, ideal for sharing with others for team-building advice.

/characters

View farmable materials in today's domains, and set up reminders to alert you when a character/weapon's materials are farmable.

/farm

Create visually stunning cards showcasing your character builds, great for sharing on social media platforms.
